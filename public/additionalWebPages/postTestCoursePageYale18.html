The Post - Test

The Post Test is a great way to see how much you have improved over the course of ONEXYS. Note this is not the same as a knowledge check that some of you have seen as you reach the end of ALEKS. In fact, to take the post test, you need to go to a brand-new ALEKS course that we've set up just for this purpose.

Here's what you need to do:

Follow the link to thePost-Test in ALEKS. This will take you to "ONEXYS 2018 Post-Test"

You will be asked to take an Initial Knowledge Check. This is the Post-Test. When you have finished the Initial Knowledge Check, you will be able to see your score. Please do not continue on to "Start my Path" .

Please complete your diagnostic by Midnight, Friday, August 10th .
