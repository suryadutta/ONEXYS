The Diagnostic

We don't want to waste your time this summer!

We want to focus only on the things that will help you the most. So, the diagnostic is the starting point for ONEXSS and does a great job of identifying your strengths as well as opportunities for growth. On top of that, it's the first (of 4) requirements to "Complete ONEXSS".

Here's what you need to do:

Follow the link to the Diagnostic Test in ALEKS. This will take you to "ONEXSS 2020 Diagnostic" course in ALEKS.

You will be asked to take an "Initial Knowledge Check."This is the diagnostic. When you have finished the diagnostic you will be able to see your score. Please do not continue on to "Start My Path" at this time.

Please complete your diagnostic by 11:59pm ET, Monday, June 15th.
