The Post - Test

The Post Test is a great way to see how much you have improved over the course of QBIT. It is also what we will use for your mathematics placement here at Trinity! Note this is not the same as a knowledge check that some of you have seen as you reach the end of ALEKS. In fact, to take the post test, you need to go to a brand-new ALEKS course that we've set up just for this purpose.



Follow the link to the "QBIT 2020 Post-Test" in ALEKS.

Click on "Enroll in Course". Next, click on "Go to the Course"

Follow instructions to ALEKS to take the Initial Knowledge Check. This is the Post-Test

Please complete the Post-Test by 1159p, Sunday, July 26th . Beginning the following week, Dr. Tunstall will follow up with information concerning potential placement changes based on scores.
