The Post - Test

The Post Test is a great way to see how much you have improved over the course of CONEX. Note this is not the same as a knowledge check that some of you have seen as you reach the end of ALEKS. In fact, to take the post test, you need to go to a brand-new ALEKS course that we've set up just for this purpose.



Follow the link to the "CONEX 2021 Post Test" in ALEKS.

Click on "Enroll in Course". Next, click on "Go to the Course"

Follow instructions to ALEKS to take the Initial Knowledge Check. This is the Post-Test
